# Social Media Platform Content Rules

## Instagram

**Caption Length**: 138–150 chars for max engagement (up to 2200 allowed but truncated at ~125)
**Structure**:
1. Line 1: Hook — bold statement, question, or emoji opener
2. Lines 2–4: Value / story / insight
3. Final line: CTA ("Save this!", "Tag a friend", "Link in bio")

**Hashtags**: 5–10, mix of:
- 2–3 broad (#skincare, #wellness)
- 3–4 mid-tier (#organicskincare, #sensitiveskincare)
- 1–2 niche/brand (#cleanbeautylife)
Place hashtags at end of caption or in first comment.

**Emoji usage**: 1–3 relevant emojis to increase click rate
**Best post times**: Tue–Fri, 9am–11am or 6pm–8pm local time

---

## Facebook

**Optimal length**: 40–80 chars for max reach; up to 500 for storytelling posts
**Structure**: Lead with the value — no fluff. Facebook algorithm rewards comments.
**CTA style**: Ask a question to spark comments ("Which do you prefer?", "Have you tried this?")
**Hashtags**: 1–2 only (overuse hurts reach on Facebook)
**Emoji**: Moderate — 1–2 max
**Best post times**: Wed–Fri, 1pm–4pm

---

## LinkedIn

**Short post** (engagement hook): 150–300 chars, punchy professional insight
**Long-form post**: 1200–1500 chars — story arc: Problem → Insight → Takeaway → CTA
**Structure for long-form**:
- Line 1: Bold hook (no "I am excited to announce")
- 3–5 short paragraphs with line breaks
- End with: question or reflection to drive comments

**Hashtags**: 3–5, professional (#Marketing, #SEO, #ContentStrategy)
**Emoji**: Minimal — 0–2, professional context only
**Best post times**: Tue–Thu, 8am–10am or 12pm

---

## Twitter / X

**Max length**: 280 characters
**Style**: Direct, punchy, opinionated or informative
**Thread option**: If topic is complex, suggest a thread (Tweet 1/5, 2/5…)
**Hashtags**: 1–2 max (more = spam signal)
**Emoji**: Optional, 1 max
**CTA**: Retweet, reply, or click link
**Best post times**: Mon–Fri, 8am–10am or 7pm–9pm

---

## TikTok (Video Script Format)

**Script length**: 150–300 words (for 30–60 sec video)
**Structure**:
```
[HOOK — 0 to 3 seconds]
One line. Pattern interrupt. Bold claim or question.
Example: "This one skincare mistake is ruining your skin."

[MAIN CONTENT — 3 to 50 seconds]
3–5 quick points or a mini story. Fast pace.
Use "Number X:" labels for listicle style.

[CTA / CLOSE — last 5 seconds]
"Follow for more tips like this."
"Comment [WORD] and I'll send you the full guide."
"Save this before you forget!"
```

**Hashtags**: 3–7, trending niche tags + broad tags
**Caption**: Keep to 100–150 chars — just a hook + main hashtags
**Tone**: Energetic, authentic, Gen-Z/Millennial friendly. Avoid corporate language.
**Trending sounds**: Note "Add a trending audio for 2–3x more reach"

---

## Cross-Platform Adaptation Tips

When repurposing content across platforms:
- LinkedIn long-form → Twitter thread → Instagram carousel caption
- Blog post → Facebook post (share link + hook) + LinkedIn insight + Twitter quote
- TikTok script → Instagram Reels script (slightly shorter)
